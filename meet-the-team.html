    WHAT WE VALUE

    As BrightBearsInfo's popularity grew over the years, so has the internal work. In 2 years, the BBI team has grown from Ling Shan and Saakshi into a team of 30+ volunteers from 4 countries. We align with the mission of BrightBearsInfo and enjoy seeing the value we provide first hand whether it's staying up till 11pm in CareerConnect with you or answering your questions on our Instagram. Your engagement makes what you see possible.
