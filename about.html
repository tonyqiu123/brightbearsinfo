    OUR JOURNEY

    "When we first co-founded BrightBearsInfo, we spent hours brainstorming ways to avoid having contact with the public. To cut a long tale short, it is difficult to create or run a non-profit organization, or any organization for that matter, without pushing yourself to develop your communication skills. At the end of the day, the success of your organization is defined by how successfully you meet the needs of your audience. Taking advantage of every opportunity to speak with your audience is critical to the organization's success in fulfilling its mission. Without effective communication skills, none of this would have been feasible. To build such skills, we have found that sharing our organization's story and goals with new people is the best place to start. New connections, relationships, and partnership opportunities will result from this effort."

    - Ling Shan & Saakshi
    Co-founders of brightbearsinfo
