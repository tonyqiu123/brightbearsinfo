    RECAP OF OUR LATEST EVENT

    May 25, 2022

    Bingo with BrightBearsInfo

    Participants joined us in an online pokemon-esque browser game called Gather Town where players can communicate with a mic and camera to other players nearby. Participants competed to fill out tasks in a Bingo sheet that involved scavenger hunts, exploring the custom BBI-themed map, and more. Whoever completed a column, row, or diagonal on their sheet won prizes including water bottles, candy and airpods!

    Over 57 participants joined us in dancing, scavenger hunts, networking, and more!

    Over 11 mystery bags were claimed with some bags worth up to $400!
